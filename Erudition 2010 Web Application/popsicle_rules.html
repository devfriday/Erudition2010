Rules :

 

  • 1. Each team will consist of two members and will be treated as single entry in the competition.
  • 2. A member cannot take part in more than one team.
  • 3. The building material shall only consist of the Popsicle sticks and white glue.
  • 4. The bridge must be able to stand on its own.
  • 5. The bridge must be able to support the load at the loading point

 

 

Testing:

  • 1. Bridges will be weighed before being loaded.
  • 2. Teams must submit their bridge 1/2 hour before the competition for inspection. Teams will be given 15 minutes to modify bridges which do not pass inspection.
  • 3. Inspection will test that the construction rules were followed and will record the mass of the bridge.
  • 4. Bridges will then be subjected to loading. Bridge failure will be considered the point at which the bridge breaks or the point at which the loading tray drops more than 5 centimeters from the unloaded position.

 

Building Materials :

    1. Glue or Adhesive.(Fevi quick is not allowed)
    2. Popsicle sticks.

Dimensions :

1. Min Span Length -45 cm - min. There will be a 45 centimeter "gap" which must be spanned by the bridge.

2. Max Span Width - 8 cm - min.

3. Min Span Height - 10 cm - min. There must be 15 centimeters between the table surface and the lowest point on the bridge span.
